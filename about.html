The Story of DJ SIX

Born and raised in Lagos, Nigeria, DJ SIX discovered his passion for music at the age of 12 when he stumbled upon his uncle's record collection. What started as a childhood obsession quickly evolved into a professional career that would take him from the streets of Lagos to the world's most prestigious stages.

Over more than 15 years in the industry, DJ SIX has become synonymous with excellence, precision, and an unmatched ability to read and move any crowd. His unique sound blends the rich rhythms of Afrobeats and Amapiano with international genres including Hip-Hop, R&B, House, and Electronic Dance Music.

DJ SIX has shared the stage with some of Africa's biggest artists including Burna Boy, Wizkid, Davido, Tiwa Savage and many more. His residencies at top Lagos nightclubs and his appearances at international festivals have cemented his reputation as one of the continent's most versatile and in-demand DJs.

Beyond the decks, DJ SIX is passionate about mentoring the next generation of Nigerian DJs and giving back to the music community that shaped him. He runs annual masterclasses and workshops for aspiring DJs across Lagos.

Whether it's an intimate wedding for 50 guests or a festival crowd of 50,000 — DJ SIX brings the same level of dedication, energy, and artistry to every single performance.

Career Journey

From Lagos to the World

2009
The Beginning
Started DJing at local Lagos events and house parties, quickly earning a reputation for his unique music selection and crowd-rocking skills.
2011
First Club Residency
Landed his first major club residency at Club Quilox in Lagos, one of Nigeria's premier nightclubs, performing weekly to thousands of guests.
2014
First Mixtape Release
Released his debut mixtape "Lagos to the World" which went viral across Nigerian social media and gained over 100,000 downloads in its first week.
2016
International Breakthrough
Performed at his first international festival in London, opening for major Afrobeats artists. This marked the beginning of his international touring career.
2018
Best DJ Award – Nigeria
Won the prestigious Nigeria Entertainment Awards for Best DJ, recognizing his outstanding contribution to the Nigerian music industry.
2020 – 2024
Global Recognition
Expanded performances to over 20 countries, collaborated with top African artists, and established himself as one of Africa's premier international DJs.
