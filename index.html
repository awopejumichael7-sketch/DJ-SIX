About DJ SIX

The Artist Behind The Decks

DJ SIX is one of Nigeria's most sought-after international DJs, renowned for his electrifying energy, impeccable music selection, and ability to read any crowd. With over 15 years of experience, he has performed at some of the world's most prestigious venues and events.

From high-energy club nights to luxurious wedding receptions, DJ SIX brings unparalleled professionalism and artistry to every performance. His unique blend of Afrobeats, Amapiano, Hip-Hop, and House music has made him a favorite among A-list celebrities and international brands.
